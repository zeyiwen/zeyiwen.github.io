Short Bio

I am a research fellow at National University of Singapore advised by Associate Professor Bingsheng He. Before working in Singapore, I was a research fellow at University of Melbourne from 2015 to 2016, completed my PhD degree at University of Melbourne in 2015, and received a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from South China University of Technology (Project 985 Universities) in 2010.

Research

  • Areas of research: System Support for Machine Learning, Parallel Computing and Data Mining.
  • Selected projects:
    • ThunderSVM
      • supports all the functionalities of LibSVM including classification, regression and distribution estimation,
      • is often 10 to 100 times faster than LibSVM,
      • has attracted 950+ stars and 140+ forks on GitHub, and
      • is built on these pieces of key related work: JMLR'18, TKDE'18 and ICDM'14.
    • ThunderGBM
      • supports classification, regression and ranking,
      • is often 10 times faster than XGBoost, LightGBM and CatBoost,
      • has attracted 370+ stars and 40+ forks on GitHub, and
      • is built on our related work: IPDPS'18 and TPDS'19.
