Short Bio

  • Dr. Zeyi Wen is Assistant Professor at the Data Science and Analytics (DSA) Thrust of Information Hub at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Guangzhou (HKUST-GZ). Before joining HKUST-GZ, he was Lecturer at The University of Western Australia (2025 QS World University Rankings 77), Research Fellow at National University of Singapore and The University of Melbourne after PhD completion at The University of Melbourne. He obtained his Bachelor's Degree from South China University of Technology. Dr. Wen was a winner of Best Paper Award at IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems (TPDS) 2019. His research work has led to open-source ML systems including ThunderGBM Star and ThunderSVM Star. He is an Action Editor (AE) of MLOSS section for the Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR).
