Short Bio

  • Dr. Zeyi Wen is Assistant Professor at the Data Science and Analytics Thrust of Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Guangzhou (HKUST-GZ). Before joining HKUST-GZ, he was a Lecturer at The University of Western Australia (UWA) from 2019 to 2022, a research fellow at National University of Singapore from 2017 to 2019, and a research fellow at The University of Melbourne after his PhD completion at The University of Melbourne in 2015. Dr. Wen is a winner of Best Paper Award (1 out of 987) at IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems (TPDS) 2019, and Best Paper Award Finalist (4 out of 329) at the 50th International Conference on Parallel Processing (ICPP) 2021. His research work has also led to open-source ML systems including ThunderGBM Star and ThunderSVM Star.
