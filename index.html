Short Bio

I am joining The University of Western Australia as a Lecturer from September 2019, and have worked as a research fellow at National University of Singapore since 2017. Before working in Singapore, I was a research fellow at The University of Melbourne from 2015 to 2016, completed my PhD degree at The University of Melbourne in 2015, and received a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from South China University of Technology in 2010.

Research Interests (publications)

  • High-Performance Computing

Selected System Projects

  • ThunderGBM
    • supports classification, regression and ranking,
    • is often 10 times faster than XGBoost, LightGBM and CatBoost,
    • has attracted 390+ stars and 50+ forks on GitHub, and
    • is built on our related work: IPDPS'18 and TPDS'19.
  • ThunderSVM
    • supports all the functionalities of LibSVM including classification, regression and distribution estimation,
    • is often 10 to 100 times faster than LibSVM,
    • has attracted 970+ stars and 140+ forks on GitHub, and
    • is built on these pieces of key related work: JMLR'18, TKDE'18 and ICDM'14.
